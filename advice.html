Experience high situation of crashings

User through the ages have experienced crashes on their computer for ages and these solution that I will provide will help you solve them.

First task is Check your temperature on your CPU or GPU. The heat is one of the common issues that involves crashes on a computer.

  • Changing the thermal paste. Changing the thermal paste will reduce the heat because there will be more contact between the layer of your CPU or GPU and heat sink. This means that there will be more dissipation of heat .
  • Cleaning the computer . Cleaning the computer will provide more air flow for the heat to dissipate from the heat sink . The heat arises due to not enough air flow being pushed through the system as one of two ways you can cool the CPU and GPU of your computer is through air
  • Changing of cooling system from air to water. Changing the your heat sink from air cooled to water cooled reduce significantly as water absorbs heat more efficiently due to their molecule structure.
